# Supabase Storage Buckets Setup

## Step 1: Navigate to Storage
1. Go to https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/rcojgwrpyurkwbvlyxmi
2. Click **Storage** in the left sidebar

## Step 2: Create Three Buckets

### Bucket 1: ra-presentation
1. Click **New bucket**
2. Name: `ra-presentation`
3. **Public bucket**: Toggle ON
4. Click **Create bucket**

### Bucket 2: correspondence
1. Click **New bucket**
2. Name: `correspondence`
3. **Public bucket**: Toggle ON
4. Click **Create bucket**

### Bucket 3: supporting-evidence
1. Click **New bucket**
2. Name: `supporting-evidence`
3. **Public bucket**: Toggle ON
4. Click **Create bucket**

## Verification
You should see all three buckets listed:
- ✅ ra-presentation
- ✅ correspondence
- ✅ supporting-evidence

## Why Public Buckets?
For demo purposes, public buckets allow direct URL access to view uploaded files. For production, you would use private buckets with signed URLs and proper access policies.
